Mixer

Red + Yellow + Blue + White == PURPLE

Drums, piping and engines are from Extra Utilities by RWTema.

Introduction

The main liquid dyes are not much good, well to be honest any good, on their own. You need to stir them up to create some pure liquid dye. This is the magic stuff, that all the machines work on.

So all you need to do it pipe red, yellow, blue and white dye in and pure dye will come out.

Inputs And Output

Mixers have the following inputs:

  • RF Power
  • Red Liquid Dye
  • Yellow Liquid Dye
  • Blue Liquid Dye
  • White Liquid Dye

Mixers have the following outputs:

  • Pure Liquid Dye

Building

The mixer is a 3x3x3 multiblock.

You need the following blocks:

  • 5 valves
  • 1 controller
  • 1 mixer
  • 20 casings
