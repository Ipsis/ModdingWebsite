Painter

Coloring Blocks?!

Drums, piping and engines are from Extra Utilities by RWTema.

Introduction

The painter is one of the most important blocks in Dyetopia.

Basically it allows you to feed items in and get the colored version out. So for example you can put any color of wool in and select different colors out.

Inputs And Output

Painters have the following inputs:

  • RF Power
  • Pure Liquid Dye
