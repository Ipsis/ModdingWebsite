Gun And Eraser

How to paint and how to cleanup afterwards

Introduction

The dye gun is basically the portable painter. It is filled with pure dye, so you can use any color you want. Well any from the 16 Minecraft ones.

Painting

To change the current color, just shift-right-click and that will cycle the colors.

To apply, just right-click an appropriate block.

Erasing

The eraser is for those special blocks, that have an uncolored version, such as hardended clay and stained clay.

To remove the color, just right-click on a block and the color will be removed.
