Plantlife

Trees, beans and pods.

Introduction

You can spend all your days, looking for flowers to squeeze the dye out of, or you can go down the Dyetopia plantlife route. This plantlife can give you dye faster then just flowers.However, these plants and trees do not exist in the world, you have to make them.

Dyemeal

Dyetopia trees dont use growth ticks or bonemeal. You need some special bonemeal called dyemeal.

Red, Yellow and Blue Dye Trees

Red Dye Tree

Yellow Dye Tree

Blue Dye Tree

These trees give you dye beans. You need to create the right sapling, plant it on the ground and then use dyemeal to make it grow. At the core of the tree you will find a dye heart. When you break this, you get dye beans which you can grow.

If you want more dye beans, grow more dye trees.

Pure Dye Trees

This tree gives you Pure Dye Tree root, which is the only things that dye beans will grow on. Create the sapling, plant it on the ground and then use dyemeal to grow it.

These saplings need 5x5 area of dirt, that is 2 deep to grow.

Dye Beans And Dye Pods

Dye beans grow on Pure Dye Tree root. So plant some of that down, once you have hacked it from the tree and then plant your beans.

The beans grow like cocoa beans. When they are mature, break them to get the dye drops out.

And yes, you can use bonemeal on them.
