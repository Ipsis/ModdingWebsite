Squeezer

Flowers In, Dye Out

Drums, piping and engines are from Extra Utilities by RWTema.

Introduction

The squeezer does pretty much as you expect. It takes items, squeezes them and product red, yellow, blue and white dye.

The amount of each dye depends on which item you put in. You can also squeeze vanilla dyes.

More importantly you can squeeze dye drop which will produce larger amounts of the approriate dye colors.

Inputs And Output

Squeezers have the following inputs:

  • RF Power
  • Squeezable items

Squeezer have the following outputs:

  • Red Liquid Dye
  • Yellow Liquid Dye
  • Blue Liquid Dye
  • White Liquid Dye

Building

The squeezer is a 3x3x3 multiblock.

You need the following blocks:

  • 4 valves
  • 1 controller
  • 1 squeezer
  • 21 casings
