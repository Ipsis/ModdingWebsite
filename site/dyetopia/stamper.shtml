Stamper

And Dye Your Heart Desires

Drums, piping and engines are from Extra Utilities by RWTema.

Introduction

The stamper has a simple purpose. Stamp out the dye that you want.

You need to feed dye blanks into the input slot of the machine and select which color of dye you want out.

As long as you have dye blanks, the machine will keep producing the selected color of dye.

Inputs And Output

Stampers have the following inputs:

  1. RF Power
  2. Pure Liquid Dye
  3. Dye Blanks

Stampers have the following outputs:

  1. Dye Chunks
